What Are Sleep Cycles and How Do They Improve Your Rest?

Getting good sleep is not only about how many hours you spend in bed, but also about the quality and the sleep cycles your body completes each night. Understanding how these cycles work can make a big difference in your daily energy, focus, and overall well-being.

How Do Sleep Cycles Work?

A full sleep cycle lasts about 90 minutes and includes several phases:

  • Light Sleep
  • Deep Sleep
  • REM Sleep (Rapid Eye Movement)

Your body repeats these cycles several times throughout the night. Waking up at the end of a cycle—rather than in the middle—can help you feel more refreshed, even if you slept fewer hours.

Tips to Improve Your Sleep

  • Go to bed and wake up at consistent times
  • Avoid screens and bright lights before bed
  • Create a dark and quiet environment
  • Exercise regularly
  • Avoid caffeine and heavy meals at night
